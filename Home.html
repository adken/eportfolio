About Me

I am Kennedy Adriko, a GIS professional, and Drone Pilot with over 4 years’ experience in the geospatial industry. My areas of expertise are developing GIS data collection tools, GIS analysis, Remote sensing, and UAV based surveys and mapping. I am currently pursuing a Copernicus Master’s in Digital Earth. My interests are in using AI such as deep learning and computer vision and drones, satellite imagery to develop actionable solutions for agriculture, natural hazards, and humanitarian crises.

Boston Biking
